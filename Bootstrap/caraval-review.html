Caraval

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

"Remember, it's only a game..." This book completely sweeps you off your feet into a world where you can't tell what is real and what is just performance. The atmosphere is lush, dark, and beautifully whimsical. Scarlett and Tella's sisterly bond drives a high-stakes, magical treasure hunt that keeps you guessing until the absolute final page. A fantastic fantasy adventure!

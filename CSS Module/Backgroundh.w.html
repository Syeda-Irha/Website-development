Wind Breaker is a high-energy action anime centered on Haruka Sakura, a misunderstood loner who transfers to Furin High to fight his way to the top. However, he discovers that the school’s notorious delinquents are actually the town's heroic protectors, known as Bofurin. The series blends intense, well-animated street fights with a heartfelt story about finding community and redefining what it means to be "strong."

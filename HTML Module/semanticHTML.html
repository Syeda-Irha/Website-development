Jujutsu Kaisen

Jujutsu Kaisen follows Yuji Itadori, a student who gains the powers of a deadly demon called Sukuna after swallowing a cursed finger. He joins a secret academy of Jujutsu Sorcerers to hunt down the remaining relics and exorcise dangerous spirits, all while facing an inevitable execution.

Jujutsu Kaisen is full of quirky secrets, like the fact that its creator stays anonymous by using a one-eyed cat avatar. The show’s strongest character, Satoru Gojo, wears a blindfold because his eyes are so powerful they would overwhelm his brain if he didn't cover them. Even the gross demon fingers the hero eats reportedly taste like soap, and many of the show's eerie settings are based on real-life haunted locations you can visit in Tokyo today.

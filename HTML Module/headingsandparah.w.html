Location:Muweilah School Zone Sharjah,UAE

Curriculum:British Cambridge / Pearson Edexcel

Year groups: FS1 to Year 13

Providence English Private School (PEPS) is a well-established British curriculum institution located in the Muweilah School Zone of Sharjah, offering an affordable yet high-quality education for students from FS1 to Year 13. Since its founding in 1991, the school has built a strong reputation for academic excellence, recently earning a "Good" rating from the Sharjah Private Education Authority (SPEA) and producing students who achieve top global marks in international examinations like the Pearson Edexcel IGCSEs. The campus is divided into specialized buildings to cater to different age groups, providing a structured environment where students follow the Cambridge International pathway toward IGCSE and A-Level qualifications. With a focus on core subjects like English and Mathematics, PEPS maintains a selective admissions process and consistently performs above global averages in international benchmarks like TIMSS, making it a preferred choice for families seeking a value-driven, co-educational British school in the UAE.

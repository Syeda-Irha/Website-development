Spy X Familiy

Spy x Family follows a master spy who forms a fake family to prevent an international war. Hilariously, he doesn't realize his "wife" is a deadly assassin and his "daughter" is a telepath who knows both their secrets. It’s a perfect mix of high-stakes missions and wholesome, chaotic family life.

Spy x Family is deeply inspired by the Cold War, with the nations of Westalis and Ostania mirroring a divided Germany. The series is full of Easter eggs, from the family name "Forger" being a pun on their fake lives to the dog, Bond, being a nod to 007. Even the manga covers are stylized, featuring each character sitting on a specific designer chair that matches their unique personality.
